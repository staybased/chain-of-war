## CONCLUSION

This completes the full unit rosters for all 10 factions in Chain of War. Each faction has exactly 10 units (100 total) with distinct mechanical identities:

- **Iron Covenant**: Elite balanced forces with leadership synergies
- **Swarm of Xeth**: Cheap hordes with adjacency bonuses  
- **Void Pharaohs**: Resurrection mechanics and high resilience
- **Emerald Tide**: Chaotic random bonuses and explosive power
- **Celestial Order**: Extreme range precision with drone support
- **Warp Touched**: High risk/reward with self-damage for power
- **Shadow Kin**: Glass cannon speed with assassination focus
- **Steel Legion**: Combined arms with vehicle synergies
- **Bone Prophets**: Mental manipulation and debuff control
- **Apex Hunters**: Mid-battle adaptation and evolution

Every unit card includes the required stats, point costs, abilities, equipment slots, and thematic flavor text. The factions each have their own playstyle while maintaining competitive balance through the rock-paper-scissors advantage web established in the design bible.
